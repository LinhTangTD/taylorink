Aftercare

Small Tattoo

After the procedure, small tattoo is wrapped with tattoo film. Healing normally occurs within 1 to 2 weeks. Following these tattoo aftercare instructions will help to ensure its vibrance and quality as it heals.

  • Wear tattoo film for next 5-7 days. It's normal to see clots, blood, and fluid under the film (so DON'T panic!)

  • Avoid scratching, direct sunlight, and swimming during healing process for best results. Wash hands thoroughly prior to touching your tattoo for any reason.

  • Watch for allergic reactions; remove film if symptoms like redness or itching appear, wash with water, pat dry with a clean cloth or towel and apply moisturizing cream

  • Continue moisturizing cream/ointment for two weeks after film removal.

Large Tattoo

For larger tattoos, the freshly tattooed area is wrapped with a protective film. However, in the case of larger tattoos, it is typically advised to remove the film within one hour after returning home.

  • Wash your hands thoroughly before removing the film and gently clean tattoo with soap and water.

  • Apply moisturizing cream for two weeks post-cleaning.

  • Avoid strenuous exercises causing excessive sweating. Strictly avoid swimming and sunbathing under direct sunlight.

  • Keep tattoo covered during healing process.
