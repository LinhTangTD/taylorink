How to request an appointment?
1
Inspiration & Photo

Share your tattoo idea and inspiration, please include reference images. Indicate where on your body you would like the tattoo. If possible, attach a clear photo of the area.

2
Personal Information

Provide your full name, age, contact number. On the day of your appointment, please bring a physical or a government-approved mobile ID.
Include any relevant details such as skin sensitivities or allergies.

3
Preferred Availability

List your preferred day of the week, dates and times for the appointment. Please mention if you have any flexibility in your schedule.

4
Shoot us a text!

Send all the above information via text or email to our studio. We will review your inquiries and get back to you with availability and further details.
