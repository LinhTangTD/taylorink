1. Initial Consultation

Text/email us your inquiries or come into the shop to discuss your tattoo.

Don't know how & where to start for your first tattoo? Simply follow our step-by-step guideline. Bring your ideas, and we'll guide you on how to make it a tattoo you'll love.

2. Book An Appointment

Once you have decided what you want, we will go through pricing and scheduling for your tattoo. After that, you can book the date and put down your deposit.

Deposit is non-refundable. However, we do try to accomodate one-time rescheduling with at least one week's notice.

3. Get Your Tattoo

When you arrive at the shop, your artist will have your design prepared. Now you settle in and enjoy getting your new tattoo.

Have questions before/after your tattoo? You might find the answers in our FAQs page. If not, feel free to reach out to us and we'll get back to you at our earliest convenience!
