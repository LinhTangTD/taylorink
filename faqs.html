Popular Questions

We crafted this page to answer many of the frequently asked questions we were asked along the way.

  • How can I book a tattoo?
    To schedule an appointment, simply follow the easy steps in Our Process . We are appointment-only services, and advance scheduling of 2 - 3 weeks is highly recommended. Get started and make your appointment today! Let the magic begin!
  • How should I prepare for my appointment?
    Prior to your appointment, please refrain from consuming alcohol. Ensure that you eat and hydrate adequately beforehand. Avoid applying lotions to your skin, as we prefer clean, dry skin for the best tattooing experience.
  • How long will my tattoo last?
    The longevity of a tattoo varies depending on factors such as skin type, location, ink quality, and aftercare. Generally, tattoos are considered permanent, but they may fade slightly over time. With proper care and sun protection, a well-done tattoo can last for many years. However, some fading is natural over the long term.
  • How should I look after my tattoo?
    See Aftercare page.
  • Can I bring a friend/family to my appointment?
    Yes! However, please be respectful of the privacy of other artists and clients in the studio during the process.
  • What is the cancellation policy?
    Our cancellation policy emphasizes that the tattoo deposit is non-refundable. We know that unexpected things can affect your plan, we try our best to accomodate one-time rescheduling, with at least 1 week's notice. If you wish to change your appointment again, we kindly request that another deposit for the flexibility. We appreciate your understanding and respect for others' time, as someone else could have booked the slot you initially reserved.
  • Can I see my design prior to my appointment?
    Yes, absolutely! We're happy to show you your design in person before your appointment. Rest assured, we take pride in our expertise and ask for your trust in our design work. Please note, for copyright reasons, we do not send designs digitally or sell them.
  • How do I take the first steps to turn my idea into a reality?
    To begin, take some time to explore and research the tattoo styles that resonate with you. Collecting reference photos can be helpful in expressing your preferences to the tattoo artist. During our consultation, we'll be more than happy to address any additional questions you may have about your new tattoo. Let's work together to bring your vision to life!
  • Can I get tattooed if I am under 18?
    No. To receive a tattoo in California, individuals must be at least 18 years of age. We ask our client to present a valid government-issued photo ID when arriving at the studio.
  • What is your tattoo style?
    At our studio, we are versatile and adept in a wide range of styles, from timeless traditional styles to cutting-edge modern trends.
